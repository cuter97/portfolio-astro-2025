# Larch Agency — Modern Marketing Agency Template

Larch Agency is a high-end marketing and creative agency website template built for studios, digital agencies, and professionals who want to showcase their work with impact, clarity, and performance.

Designed with a cinematic visual language and a strong focus on motion and storytelling, Larch Agency combines bold layouts, smooth GSAP animations, and a clean content structure to guide visitors through services, projects, and conversion points effortlessly.

![Screenshot of Larch website](/img/img-6.webp)

## Project Overview

This template delivers a premium agency presence that feels modern, confident, and production-ready from day one. Every section is carefully designed to communicate value, showcase expertise, and convert visitors into leads.

## Technology Stack

- **Astro 5** — Core framework for maximum performance and static-first architecture
- **React Islands** — Selective interactivity for dynamic components while keeping client-side JavaScript minimal
- **Tailwind CSS v4** — Utility-first styling for consistent design tokens and responsive layouts
- **TypeScript** — Type-safe configuration and predictable component behavior
- **GSAP** — Professional animations and scroll-triggered interactions
- **Lenis** — Smooth scrolling experience
- **Resend** — Secure contact form with email delivery
- **Upstash** — Server-side rate limiting for anti-spam protection
